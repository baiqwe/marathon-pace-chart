Frequently Asked Questions

Before reaching out, you might find the answer to your question below:

Are your pace charts accurate?

Yes, our pace charts are calculated with precision. However, please note that actual race performance can vary based on factors like terrain, weather, and individual fitness levels.

Can I use your pace charts for training?

Absolutely! Our pace charts are designed to help you plan both your races and training runs. Use them to set goals and maintain consistent pacing during your workouts.

Is Marathon Pace Chart free to use?

Yes, all our tools and resources are completely free. We're supported by advertising to keep our content accessible to all runners.

Can I suggest new features or content?

We welcome all suggestions! Please use the contact form above or email us directly with your ideas. We're always looking to improve our website based on user feedback.

Do you offer personalized training plans?

Currently, we provide general pace charts and training information. For personalized training plans, we recommend consulting with a certified running coach.
