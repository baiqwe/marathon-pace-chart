How Far Is a Marathon? How Many Miles Is a Half Marathon

Marathon distance: 26.2 miles (42.195 km). Half marathon distance: 13.1 miles (21.0975 km).

Origins of the distances

Marathon distance traces to the modern Olympic Games and the 1908 London course; the half marathon (21.0975 km) is half that length, standardized for certification and record-keeping.

Pacing references

  • 2 hour half marathon pace ≈ 5:41 min/km (9:09 min/mile).
  • 3 hour marathon pace ≈ 4:16 min/km (6:52 min/mile).
  • 4 hour marathon pace ≈ 5:41 min/km (9:09 min/mile).
